WHAT DRIVES US

ONLINE QUIZ

 

Our Online Quiz system is developed for the teachers who are interested in taking online quizzes. Teachers can create quizzes in different categories where each category represents a concept. Instantaneous results to both the students and the teachers are provided.





ASSIGNMENT SUBMISSIONS

 

Our Online Assignment Submission System allows students to submit the given assignments. Our LMS is accessible through the web, there is no need to come to the University for submitting assignments.

SUPPORT

 

Our Center for Learning Design Team is always there for the students in case of any problem. We are dedicated towards helping students in any way we can. You can email us at stenehardy10@gmail.com. Every query is solved within 24 working hours (Saturdays, Sundays and holidays excluded).




Responsive Design

 

The theme designed by the Center for Learning Design team is responsive so that it can be accessed from your laptop, tablet or cell phone easily. The warm red colour scheme gives the website a vibrant look and feel.
